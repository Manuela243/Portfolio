About Me

I still remember the time in primary school when we started working with HTML. It seemed interesting as to how writing some specific words and inbetween adding your own, you get a complete website that might be used one day by literary everyone in the whole world!
Life usually takes you on different paths than the one you want, so last year I chose to make a difference. I want to change my life. I found out my passion for development has grown stronger than ever.
I will have masters in law, but coding is something I love with all my heart.
I started to self-teach HTML, CSS and Javascript, after a long search over the internet for a good roadmap to follow. I learned from FreeCodeCamp, The Odins Project, The Geeks and many more sites, and of course, YouTube came in handy.
Whenever I have free time after work, I try to code and learn more and more. I can not wait to see what other programming languages I will be able to learn and use with ease.
But, it is not easy to do it all alone, that is why I am looking for an opportunitey to find a place where I can grow with others, get guidance that I need, and that can be a bootcamp or a full-time job. I want to show that I am ready and not afraid to work hard everyday in order to achive what I dream of. I want to be able to not only code in my free time, but to code alongside people that share the same passion as I do.
