About Me

I’m someone who’s always been driven by curiosity and a love for learning—and lately, that path has led me to something I’ve completely fallen for: coding and web development. 💻

Even though I don’t come from a technical background, that hasn’t held me back. If anything, it’s pushed me to dive in even deeper. I’ve been teaching myself the basics—HTML, CSS, and JavaScript 🧱🎨⚡️—and the more I learn, the more excited I get about where this could go.

I’m full of ideas 💡, but I know ideas need more than passion—they need support, guidance, and knowledge to come to life. That’s why I’m looking to grow in an environment where I can learn from experienced developers, be mentored, and keep challenging myself every day. You can’t build something great alone—and I’m ready to be part of a team that helps each other grow. 👥🌱

For me, every project is a chance to get better, to explore new ways of thinking, and to bring something meaningful into the world. I can’t wait to see where this journey takes me—and I’m all in. 💪✨
